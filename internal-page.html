HAIR & BEAUTY BY GK LONDON

Professional Hair Salon

GK London is a creative collaboration founded by London’s best the Fashion industry has to offer, who noticed a missing niche in the London Fashion industry – We have created a fun-filled unique Hair, Beauty & photography Studio without pretense, but filled with a sense of family, talent, and a great vibe. We at GK London realize that what is unique about you isn’t always obvious – the extraordinary can often be hidden from view. We are dedicated to providing you with an unmatched experience that allows you to express who you are as individual; all of our creations are inspired by you as a person. Our creative team are amongst the best in the business. All are carefully selected for their professionalism and extensive experience working within the fashion industry. This ensures every one of our clients enjoy the very best possible service. When not applying their trade at GK London, our creative team can often be found working among top fashion names including A-list celebrities, top international and UK based glossies as well as New York/London/Paris Fashion Week.
